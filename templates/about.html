About 
CleanEnergy.

Clean Energy is an energy company based in Iceland with locations on three continents and continuing to grow. Our mission is to promote the use of renewable energy and show people that a future without fossil fuels is not only possible but also closer than we'd like to think and the sooner we make the switch, the better it is for our and our planet's health. Our work also expands to aiding eco non-profit organizations in their mission to help save our planet's ecosystems as we donate 20% of our profits every month.

Our Work

Donating to non-profits

We donate 20% of our profits each month to local organizations in Europe and Latin America who are doing the best they can to preserve the native habitats of their countries from human destruction but also Climate Change. These donations play a crucial part in preserving the biodiversity of countries like Costa Rica, Argentina, Norway, Iceland, and Ireland our work wouldn't carry the same weight as it does now.

Changing minds

We work not only to change individual people's minds but also the minds of whole companies. We've contacted numerous companies around the world and managed to turn them green! This is a very important step to take seeing as the everyday homes of regular people don't have as big of an impact on the climate as big corporations do.
